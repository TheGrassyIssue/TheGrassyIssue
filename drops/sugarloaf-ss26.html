Sugarloaf Social Club just dropped nine pieces and opened a showroom they're calling the Locker Room.

Sugarloaf Social Club Spring/Summer 2026 9 Pieces · 25 Variations Available Now
SSC

Sugarloaf Social Club has been quietly becoming the connective tissue of indie golf. They've already collaborated with Sentinel, MacKenzie, Students Golf, and Devereux — and now they've dropped their own SS26 lineup with nine core pieces across twenty-five variations. The collection is available now, alongside a new physical showroom in McLean, Virginia they're calling the SSC Locker Room.

The range is restrained in the way that only a brand with nothing to prove can be. A cotton cap, a button-down that splits the difference between clubhouse and dinner reservation, a lightweight anorak with sport-utility DNA, and shorts cut clean enough to not need a belt. There's also a collab Sentinel Golf rangefinder case tucked into the accessories, because of course there is.

If Sugarloaf has a design philosophy, it's this: golf clothes should look like they were designed by someone who also owns non-golf clothes. The SS26 collection is their clearest statement of that idea yet — laid back, thoughtful, and fully in its lane without ever announcing the lane exists.
