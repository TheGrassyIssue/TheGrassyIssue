Manors drops the SS26 Press Room capsule.

Manors Spring/Summer 2026 $85 – $285 Available Now
Manors

Manors' SS26 Press Room capsule is the kind of collection that makes you realize most golf brands are trying too hard. Unlined cotton, waxed hems, four hidden pockets for tees, scorecards, receipts, and whatever else accumulates during eighteen holes and the bar tab after.

The palette runs cream, olive, and a washed navy that looks like it's already been broken in. No logos bigger than a thumbprint. The trousers have a genuine crease — the kind you'd see in a 1970s press room photo — and the jackets sit somewhere between workwear and something you'd wear to a gallery opening in Marfa.

This is Manors doing what they do best: making clothes that work on the course without screaming that they were made for the course. The press room jacket ($185) is the standout. The rest of the capsule follows the same logic — restrained, specific, and quietly expensive.
