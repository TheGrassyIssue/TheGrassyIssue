Manors' Club Collection Tears Down the Velvet Rope

Manors dropped The Club Collection in late February, imagining a private members' club where the dress code got lost in the post. The standout is the Outsider Polartec Hoodie — Power Dry fabric that handles British spring, which is to say sideways rain and existential doubt. New colorways on the Course Polo and GOAT Pique round out the range, plus an SS26 run hitting SSENSE for anyone who wants their golf kit curated alongside Rick Owens.
