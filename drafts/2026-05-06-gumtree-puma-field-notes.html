Gumtree Golf & Nature Club x PUMA — "Field Notes" Collection

PUMA and Gumtree Golf dropped the Field Notes collection on April 22, built around Rocky Mountain earth tones and the radical idea that golf doesn't need to move faster. The range runs from a quarter-zip in Warm White ($130) to graphic tees in styles called Pardoides and Wild Flower ($55), with a Dark Sage five-panel cap ($40) that looks like it was dyed in a creek bed. A golf collab that asks you to slow down and watch a bird — genuinely the most subversive thing PUMA has done in years.

$40–$130
