Gumtree Golf & Nature Club x PUMA — Field Notes Collection

PUMA and Gumtree Golf dropped the Field Notes collection on April 22, built around the radical idea that golf doesn't need to be played at a dead sprint. The range pulls its palette from the Rocky Mountains — sandstone polos, warm-white quarter-zips, Helsinki G sneakers in two earth tones — all in natural materials that want you to slow down and look at a bird or something. A collection that pairs better with binoculars than a launch monitor.
