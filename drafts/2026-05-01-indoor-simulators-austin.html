7 Indoor Simulators for Austin's Gross Rainy Days
Three TrackMan 4 bays in a converted warehouse south of Slaughter. Southside Golf Co is the most low-key sim spot in Austin — no velvet ropes, no $18 cocktails, just clean bays with tour-level radar and a lounge with pool tables. PGA instruction available, leagues running, walk-ins welcome when a bay's open. The vibe is closer to a buddy's garage setup than a golf bar, and that's the whole point.
