Malbon x Gap: 33 Pieces, a Custom PAR Logo, and Jesper Parnevik's Kids

Malbon and Gap are dropping a 33-piece collection on June 6 that swaps the arch for a custom PAR logo, which is exactly the kind of dad-pun energy both brands were born to deliver.
