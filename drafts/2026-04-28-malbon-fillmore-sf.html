Malbon Golf Opens on Fillmore Street, San Francisco

Malbon quietly opened a storefront on the corner of Pine and Fillmore in SF, taking over the old Byredo space with the kind of energy that says "we play 18, then we eat sourdough." The store anchors around the Buckets Club — a $300/year membership that gets you into tournaments at Torrey Pines, Pasatiempo, and beyond, which is a better deal than most country club initiation fees by roughly five zeros. Opening party was April 2nd, so the paint is barely dry.
