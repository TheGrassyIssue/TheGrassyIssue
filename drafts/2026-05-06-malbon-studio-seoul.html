Malbon Golf — Studio Seoul SS26

Malbon's SS26 capsule borrows from Seoul's obsession with technical garments that don't announce themselves, landing on hybrid outerwear and layered essentials with sharper proportions and pockets that feel considered. Sunjae Im gives the whole thing on-course credibility. Korean design language applied to golf apparel — which, if you think about it, was overdue by about a decade.
