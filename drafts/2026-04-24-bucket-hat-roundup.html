8 Bucket Hats Worth the Brim

The bucket hat is having its quiet moment on the course — no logos screaming from the crown, no performance fabric monologues on the hang tag. Just a wide brim, some shade, and the vague suggestion that you know what you're doing out there. We pulled eight from four brands that get it right.

Manors' Greenskeeper is recycled fabric with a wide brim that matches their jacket. Malbon covers the range from a clean $58 script logo to the reversible Velasco at $88. Students Golf went full boonie with a screen-printed marble camo that shouldn't work but does. And Birds of Condor — the Australian brand that treats golf hats like an art form — brings three: a reversible Swing Kings, a corduroy Rage, and the Fore Tiger with its embroidered big cat. Price range across the board: $58–$88 USD, $40–$60 AUD.
