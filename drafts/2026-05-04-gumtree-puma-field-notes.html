Gumtree Golf & Nature Club x PUMA — Field Notes Collection

PUMA and Gumtree just dropped a collection that tells you to slow down and look at the mountains, which is an unusual ask from a performance brand. The Field Notes range runs landscape-palette polos, natural-material layers, and a Rocky Mountain color story across on-and-off-course silhouettes. It is very possible to spend an entire round identifying wildflowers instead of reading putts, and this kit would support that decision.

Price: Varies — available now at retail and pumagolf.com
