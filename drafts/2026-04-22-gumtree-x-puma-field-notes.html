Gumtree Golf x PUMA: The Field Notes Collection Lands

Gumtree Golf & Nature Club teamed with PUMA on the Field Notes collection, which is what happens when a brand that hand-sews headcovers from vintage fishing waders meets a company that makes sneakers for Rickie Fowler. The capsule includes polos and gear built around Gumtree's nature-first ethos. Two percent of every Gumtree purchase still goes toward pulling CO₂ out of the atmosphere, which is at least one stroke better than most of us manage on a par 3.
